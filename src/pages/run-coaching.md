## Break Free from the Desk

I've been there — hours at a desk, debugging code, shipping features, feeling the mental fog and physical stiffness that comes with a sedentary tech lifestyle. Running changed everything for me, and I want to help you experience that transformation too.

## Why Running for Tech Workers?

As developers and knowledge workers, we face unique challenges:

- **Mental Clarity** - Break through afternoon slumps and decision fatigue
- **Stress Relief** - Process work challenges while logging miles instead of doom scrolling
- **Physical Health** - Combat the effects of sitting, improve posture and energy
- **Better Sleep** - Reset your circadian rhythm for deeper, more restorative rest
- **Sustainable Productivity** - Build endurance that translates to longer, more focused work sessions

## I Get the Struggle

The tight deadlines. The back-to-back meetings. The "just one more commit" that turns into three more hours at your desk. The guilt of choosing a run over shipping a feature. I understand these trade-offs because I've lived them.

Running isn't about adding more stress — it's about creating space for your mind and body to recover so you can show up better in your work and life.
